Help: Idea date/time

For each idea you enter you can optionally add a start and/or end date.

Dates may be entered in a number of formats, for example:

  • 14 May 2008
  • 14-05-2008
  • 14 May 2008 9:00
  • 14 May 2008 9:00PM
  • 14-05-2008 9:00PM
  • 9:00
  • 14 May
  • wed
  • wed 9:00

Note that the following formats would not be accepted:

  • 14 05 2008
  • 14/05/2008
  • 14 05 2008 9:00
  • 14/05/2008 9:00
  • 14-05
