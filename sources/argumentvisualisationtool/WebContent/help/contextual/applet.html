Connection Network Graph

Background:

    L-drag to pan
    R-click to fit network on screen (Apple-Click on Macs)
    R-drag to zoom in/out (Apple-Drag on Macs)

Ideas:

    L-click to highlight what's connected
    L-click to view/edit its profile
    Duplicate Ideas (created by >1 user) have a border
    L-click duplicate Ideas to view profiles in Idea List

Connections:

    Mouse over blobs to view an Idea's role
    L-click to edit/delete
